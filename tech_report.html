iPhone X

The iPhone X is the latest iPhone. The phone was relased on November 3rd, 2017. The iPhone X comes with many new features the biggest being them taking away the home button. Another big feature that was added to the iPhone X was face recognition. There was a lot of controversy when they announced the iPhone X. Alot of people thought that by not having a home button the phone wouldnt be the same compared to the older phones.
